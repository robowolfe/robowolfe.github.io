Looking Back
It provides wisdom on how to live a Godly lifestyle, almost a contrast to the historical books about Israel and everything not to do. It also shows the intention and gives some of the God’s reasoning behind the laws given to Israel as well.
Looking Forward
It general, points to the fact that we will never follow all the wisdom in these books, and that we need Someone who can. Directly, multiple Psalms prophecy to Christ and Ecclesiastes shows the fallenness of the world and the need for Christ to mend it.
